What do college students, sailors in the 18th century, and Mayan priests all have in common?

Tea

18th century sailors would often consume hot black tea mixed with sugar and rum.

Tea contains ~40 mg of caffeine per cup.

Coffee

Coffee is one of the most popular sources of caffeine amongst college students

Coffee contains ~95 mg of caffeine per cup.

Cacao

For the Ancient Maya, cacao was an extremely valuable commodity for trade.

Cacao contains ~20 mg of caffeine per serving.
